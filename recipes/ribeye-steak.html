Ribeye Steak

Description

A very easy recipe

Ingredients

  • ribeye steak
  • salt
  • black pepper
  • butter

Steps

  1. Pat the steak dry with paper towels.
  2. Season the steak with salt and pepper.
  3. Sear the steak on medium-high heat for 2 minutes on each side.
  4. Add butter into the pan and place it in a prehaeted oven at 350 degrees fahrenheit for 3-4 minutes.
  5. Let it rest for 5 minutes.
